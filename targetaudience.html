Target Audience

We are looking for adventurous people that are willing to have great experiences. those who are willing to explore beyond the unknown to find new and better places and businesses. If you don't settle for basic, common and traditional touristic destinations like Rio or São Paulo. You are part of our target public.


Personas

Bastos is 25 years old, lives in Florianópolis (SC) and works in the marketing area of ​​a technology company. His main goal is to visit new places to reduce his stress by doing something he really enjoys.
For being young and has only been in his company for a short time, he has a limited budget to travel, preferring to pay a monthly plan. He is a bit insecure to go to a violent place.

Esther, the cautious one: she is already thinking about retirement Esther is 57 years old, an architect and works as a freelancer. Born in Minas Gerais, she has 3 children, all grown ups , she's divorced and lives by herself. As Esther works on her own, she plans to save part of her monthly income, which is around 3,000 reais, and make a long-term investment in order to guarantee a comfortable retirement where she can travel. Ester wants to retire at age 60 and is looking for a good and safe destination that will help her have good time and make some memories, as she understands very little about financial matters.
