A little about our city

Vila Velha is a Brazilian municipality located on the coast of the state of Espírito Santo, in the Southeast Region of the country. It belongs to the Metropolitan Region of Vitória and occupies an area of ​​209,965 km², of which 54.57 km² are in the urban perimeter, and the population in 2021 was estimated by the IBGE at 508,655 inhabitants, which makes the municipality the second most populous in Espírito Santo Santo, behind only Serra.
